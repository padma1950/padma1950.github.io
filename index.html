Discover our story and see why we’re dedicated to making data center site selection smarter for everyone.

What We Do

Trinity leverages geospatial mapping and AI-driven models to streamline the data center site selection process. Our platform enables clients to locate optimal sites quickly and efficiently, combining crucial factors such as proximity to power sources (like electric grids, renewable energy sources, nuclear power, natural gas, microgrids, and battery storage solutions), fiber optic networks, and zoning regulations, all on a single map interface. By integrating these elements, Trinity provides a comprehensive solution that saves time and resources, allowing clients to make informed, data-driven decisions with ease.

What We Provide
